Karaoke for a Cause!

We want to start our first year off with a bang, or rather a WOOF! As the Chicago Bears said in 1985, “We’re not doing this because we’re greedy, The Bears are doing it to feed the needy” - ‘85 Super Bowl Shuffle.

Sing to Save the Pups

Ride guilt-free knowing that a portion of the proceeds will be given to our beneficiary, Wright-Way Rescue. Boataoke 2018 is an effort to raise money for Wright-Way Rescue, a no-kill animal shelter and rescue facility that provides treatments and finds the FURever homes of thousands of cats and dogs per year.

Venue information

Docked at River City Marina, Chicago Lakefront Cruises operates the "Summer of George". The 100 foot vessel was designed and built as a party cruise boat with an enclosed main deck, a full service bar and a dance floor to keep you entertained while enjoying 360 degree views of the beautiful Chicago skyline. Paid parking is available at the River City Marina (spaces limited).

We will begin boarding 30-minutes prior to the event, so please arrive early to ensure an on time departure.
