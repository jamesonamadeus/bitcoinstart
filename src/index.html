Booze Cruise + Karaoke = Boat aoke

2018 marks the inaugural year for Chicago’s first official Karaoke Party Boat!

SING TO SAVE THE PUPS is the campaign theme for this year! Thus, a portion of proceeds will be donated to the non-profit organization, Wright-Way Rescue!

These three-hour events on Chicago Lakefront Cruises' Summer of George will give everyone the opportunity to showcase their skills (or lack of) for all of Chicago to see! We will be a Loud, Bright, and Excited veseel cruising downt eh Chicago River that will have on-lookers saying "I want to do that!"

The events will take place on June 29th, July 19th, August 3rd, and September 21st.
