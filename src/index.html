Let it begin!

Join us for the inaugural summer of Chicago’s First Official Karaoke Party Boat!

The three-hour voyage on Chicago Lakefront Cruises’ Summer of George will have you singing your hearts out while engulfed in the city’s lights! The boat will cruise from the South Loop, down the river, and out on to the Lake. So grab your friends, have some drinks, and showcase your singing skills (or lack thereof).
