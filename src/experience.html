Summer 2018 marks the inaugural year of Chicago’s first official karaoke party boat, Boataoke.

Enjoy singing under the city lights as the 100 foot vessel cruises down the Chicago River and out to the Lake!

Sing to Save the Pups! Boataoke 2018 is an effort to raise money for Wright-Way Rescue, a no-kill animal shelter and rescue facility that provides treatments and finds the FURever homes of thousands of cats and dogs per year.
